barites

At Rajlaxmi Minchem, we offer premium barytes, a barium sulfate mineral essential in various industrial applications. Barites are widely used in the oil and gas, paint, plastics, rubber, and pharmaceutical industries. Our barites are known for their high specific gravity, chemical inertness, and consistent particle size.

Rajlaxmi Minchem is dedicated to providing high-quality barytes that meet the specific needs of our customers. Rely on us for dependable, top-grade barytes products designed to optimize your industrial processes.

Applications

Oil and gas drilling (drilling mud)
Paints and coatings
Plastics manufacturing
Rubber products
Pharmaceutical formulations
