About Us

Welcome to Rajlaxmi Minchem, a leader in the mineral industry with over 25 years of experience. Established in 1984 and ISO 9001: 2008 certified, we have grown from humble beginnings as a trading company to owning extensive mining and processing facilities in Udaipur, Rajasthan.

Our Journey

Starting with just trading, we have evolved into a fully integrated company with our own mines and processing plants. Located in Mavli, Udaipur, our mining operations cover up to 100 hectares, and our grinding units boast a capacity of over 10,000 metric tons per month. This expansion has been driven by our commitment to integrity and continuous innovation.

Our Products

Rajlaxmi Minchem offers a well-diversified portfolio of products including talc, dolomite, calcite, silica, barites, and clay. These minerals have various applications across multiple industries, thanks to our ability to manufacture them to precise customer specifications. We produce minerals ranging from 5 microns to 100 microns, meeting exact physical properties such as brightness, whiteness (CIE, ISO), color values (l, a, b), shade, residue on sieves, particle size analysis, bulk density, flow point, oil absorption, refractive index, moisture content, loss on ignition, viscosity, abrasion, opacity, reducing strength, and chemical properties like CaCO3 content, MgCO3 content, Al2O3 content, Fe2O3 content, heavy metals, conductivity, pH value, and other parameters specified by our esteemed customers.

Commitment to Quality and Sustainability

We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art processing techniques and environmentally friendly mining practices. Our quality management system ensures that every product we deliver meets the highest standards, and our commitment to sustainability ensures that our operations have minimal environmental impact.

Leadership and Values

Our values are reflected in our leadership and professional teams. We believe in integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction, which have been the cornerstones of our growth. Our strong track record of customer satisfaction speaks volumes about our reliability and commitment to providing high-quality talc products at competitive prices.

Vision and Mission

Vision: To be a global leader in the mineral industry, recognized for our quality, sustainability, and innovation.

Mission: To continuously innovate and improve our processes, ensuring the highest quality products and services that exceed our customers' expectations, while fostering a sustainable and ethical business environment.

Leading the Market

As the leading producer and exporter of talc from Udaipur, Rajasthan, we supply our products to both domestic and international markets. Our reputation as a reliable supplier is built on our consistent quality and customer-centric approach.

The Minerals of Udaipur

Udaipur is renowned for its rich mineral resources. The region's geology provides an abundant supply of high-quality talc, dolomite, calcite, silica, barites, and clay, which are integral to various industrial applications. Our mines tap into these resources, ensuring that we can deliver top-quality minerals to our clients worldwide.
